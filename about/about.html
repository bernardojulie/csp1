What we do

At That Julie Baked we make delicious cookies, cakes and other pastries that everyone will surely love. Everything is done from scratch. We use only premium ingredients and we try to avoid using artificial flavors and colors.

About Julie

Julie loves to watch cooking shows, and dreams of having her own coffee shop someday.
